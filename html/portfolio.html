We are foodpanda

foodpanda is one of the world’s largest online food ordering marketplaces. We launched our first market, Singapore, in 2012 and are headquartered in Berlin. Currently we are active across Asia, Eastern Europe, Latin America, and the Middle East, employing more than 3,700 people worldwide.In June 2014 the foodpanda group won the European Tech Startup Award as best E-Commerce Start-Up. The company has raised over US $300 Million from various investors since its launch in 2012.
